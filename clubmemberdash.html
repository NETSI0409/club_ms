About Us

Rotaract Club of R.V. College of Engineering is an institution-based club in R.I. District -3190, sponsored by Rotary Bangalore South. The club takes pride in having a record of an annual 1000+ registrations every year and thus being the biggest Rotaract club in South East Asia and one of the largest in the world. The club has successfully completed over 1400 projects till date.
